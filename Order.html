Introduction to my online restaurant

I am not just only want to share the knowledge and the recipes of foods from different countries. I also want to share the taste of them. Therefore, I start my online restaurant in my private kitchen and cook dishes with heart and sell them.

This page provides a online food ordering and delivery services to you. You can follow the below instuctions to order the foods.

  1. Click the checkbox to choose which foods you want to buy
  2. Enter the quantity of the item you want to buy
  3. Choose the delivery location of your address
  4. Leave your delivery address and contact number
  5. The summary of your order will be shown

Also, there is a special discount if your order amount is over a specific value. The detail is shown below:

  • for total food amount between $50 and $80: 10% discount
  • for total food amount between $81 and $100: 15% discount
  • for total food amount over $100: 20% discount

If you have further questions, you can look at the frequently asked questions. It may help you.

Frequently Asked Questions

When can I receive the delivery?
The delivery time depends on the delivery address. We make a promise that your ordered foods will be delivered within one and a half hour after ordering. If we fail to deliver the food within one and a half hour, you can make a full refund.
How do I pay for the bill?
There are two payment methods which you can use to make the payment:
  1. use your online banking to pay
  2. use credit card/debit card to pay
How can i refund if i cancel the order?
You can only refund it if there is problem with the foods or the delivery. For applying the refund, you can contact me by my phone number or email. The fund will be return to your bank account or credit/debit card within a serveral days.
