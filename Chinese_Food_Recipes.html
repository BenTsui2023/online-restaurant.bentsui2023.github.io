Recipe for Kung Pao Chicken

Kung Pao Chicken is a well-known Sichuan cuisine.It made with stir-fried chicken, chilies, and peanuts. Because of its sweet and sour favour, Kung Pao Chicken usually served with a bowl of white rice and chinese people love it.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mixing half of the wine, soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch with water in a cup and set it aside.
  2. Mixing another half of wine, soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch with water in another cup. Then, adding hot chile paste, water chestnuts, peanuts, green onions, garlic, sugar, and white vinegar to the cup.
  3. Cooking the boneless chicken breast in a skillet with medium heat until aromatic.
  4. Adding the prepared mixtures to the skillet and mix it. Then, heat it until the sauce becomes thick.
Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity
cornstarch 2 tablespoons
white wine 2 tablespoons
water chestnuts 8 ounce
soy sauce 2 tablespoons
sesame oil 2 tablespoons
peanut 4 ounces
hot chile paste 1 ounce
sugar 2 teaspoons
white vinegar 1 teaspoon
boneless chicken breast 1 pound
garlic 1 tablespoo
green onions 4 pieces

Recipe for Sweet and Sour Pork

Sweet and Sour Pork is a well-known Guangdong cuisine. Also, it is a perfect dish served with white rice because of its sweet and spur favour

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mixing 1.5 cups of water with sugar and vinegar in a pan and heat it to until boiling, then set aside. This is our sweet and sour sauce.
  2. Mixing flour, oil, cornstarch, egg, salt, and white pepper in a bowl and whisk it with 1 cup of water. Then, adding boneless chicken breast to the mixture and stir it to coat the chicken breast.
  3. Frying the chicken breast with hot oil with 180 degrees celsius about 10 minutes.
  4. Cooking the fried chicken breast with green peppers, pineapple chunks and sweet and sour sauce.
Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity
pineapple chunks 8 ounce
white sugar 12 teaspoons
white vinegar 6 teaspoons
cornstarch 4 tablespoons
flour 2 cups
egg 1 piece
salt half tablespoon
white pepper half tablespoon
boneless chicken breast 10 pieces
vegetable oil 4 cups
green bell pepper 2 pieces

Recipe for Yangzhou Fried Rice

Yangzhou fried rice is the most famous Chinese fired rice in China and served in many Chinese restaurants around the world. It is a simple dish and easy for beginner to learn cooking fired rice.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat the minced garlic with oil in the pan. Then add the shrimps to cook until the colour of shrimps because red and set aside.
  2. Beat and pour the eggs to a mixture and cook it. Then, dividing it into small pieces of eggs and mix with the rice.
  3. Mixing the rice with other ingredients, soy sauce, sugar, and salt.
  4. Finally, adding pork and shrimps and cook for around 5 minutes.Then, mixing it with green onions and cook for around 1 minute.
Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity
cooked white rice 5 cups
pork 1 cup
soy sauce 2 tablespoons
salt 2 tablespoons
shrimps 8 pieces
green peas 8 tablespoons
green onion 6 tablespoons
eggs 2 pieces
sugar 1 teaspoon
minced garlic 1 teaspoon
vegetable oil 3 teaspoon
