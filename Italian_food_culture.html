Italian Food Culture

Features of Italian Food Culture

Italian food focuses on using fresh and high quality ingredients. In Italy, they use locally grown ingredients such as fresh tomato, eggplant and cheese to make dishes rather than using pre-packaged food.

Also, Italian cuisine's recipes are usually simple but not complex. They usually are made with little ingredients and simple methods. It is because Italian wants to show the freshness of the ingredients.

Pasta, Pizza and the daily meal of Italian

In Italy, people cannot live without Pasta and Pizza! Pizza is the most traditional foods in Italy. The traditional pizza is fresh, simple and can be made with different ingredients.

Pasta is another important dish for Italian. According to the size and shape of the pasta, there are many types of pasta such as fettuccine, spaghetti, penne, and tagliatelle.
