Introduction

Purpose of the website

Welcome to my website. This website is an online restaurant and also a knowledge sharing platform.

To share and promote different food cultures around the world is the main purpose of this website. In addition to the food cultures, this website also share recipes of Chinese, Japanese, Korean and western food, and sell foods.

If you are someone who wants to learn how to cook foods from different countries or wants to try foods of different countries, this website may help you. You can order foods and we provide delivery service to you.

About myself

My name is Ben. I have lived in Hong Kong for many years. Living in Hong Kong, I was able to eat foods from different countries so i was familiar with foods from different countries such as Japan, England, Korean and China. Also, cooking is one of my hobbies and i am always in the kitchen cooking with my family. Therefore, having a restaurant is my dream and this website is my first step to pursue my dream.

Besides, I believe that food is not just for people to adopt and maintain healthy. People are connected with their culture. Having different cultural backgrounds, they use different ingredients, methods and techniques to make different meals.
